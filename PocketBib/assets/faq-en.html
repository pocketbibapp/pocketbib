Administrator Help

How can I login?
- Go to the login screen.
- Type in your email address and password.
- You can stay logged in even if you close the app by pressing "Keep me loged in".
- You are now logged in.

How can I log out?
- Open up the menu either seen in the action bar or by pressing the MENU button of your phone. - Click on "Logout".
- Confirm that you want to log out.
- You are now logged out. To use the app again, please login.(How Can I login?)
- Do you want to be deleted from the system? -> Please contact an administrator.

How can I change my password?
- Are you logged in? If not, please login (How Can I login?)
- Open up the menu either seen in the action bar or by pressing the MENU button of your phone.
- Click on "My Account".
- Click on the button "Change Password".

Which information is saved about me?
In this app these following informations are saved about you:
- Your email address
- Your first name and second name
- Your building, room and phone number (optional)
- Your password
- Your status (active/inactive)
- The time when you borrow and return a book.
- All your comments

- Click on the search icon, which can be seen on the action bar.
- You can search for a single word, use the extended search (search for author, title, ISBN...), use speech search (Can I search for a book by speech search?) or activate the barcode scanner (How do I search using the barcode scanner?).

Can I search for a book by speech search?
- This is only possible if this feature is provided by your phone.
- Click on the search icon and after that on the microphone symbol.
- Speak your search results to your phone when promted and wait for the search results.

How do I borrow a book?
- Are you logged in? If not, please login (How Can I login?)
- Search for the book (How do I search for a book?) and click on the correct one.
- On the Book information page you can see if the book is available or not. If it is available, click "borrow book".
- This book will now be seen on your list of leaned books.

What can I do if my book is not available?
- Are you logged in? If not, please login (How Can I login?)
- Click on the button "Not available".
- Choose a user from the list of users currently borrowing a copy of this book.
- Pick your email client.
- You can either use the default text or write your own email to contact the user.

How can I cite a book using BibTeX?
1. Solution (single book)
- Search for the book (How do I search for a book?) and click on the correct one.
- Click on "Export BibTex" in the shown actions.
- Choose the application where you want to save the citation.

2. Solution (multiple books)
- Go to the start page where you can see all of your borrowed items.
- Longpress on an item.
- Now select all of the items you want to cite.
- Select the "BibTeX" icon in the action bar.
- Choose the application where you want to save the citation.

How can I rate a book?
- Are you logged in? If not, please login (How Can I login?)
- Search for the book (How do I search for a book?) and click on the correct one.
- Click on the action "Display all comments.".
- Click on the button "Own rating".
- You can now select your rating (between 1 and 5 stars) and write a comment.
- Submit it when finished.
